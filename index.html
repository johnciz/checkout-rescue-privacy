Checkout Rescue AI — Privacy Statement

Phase 16D_1
Last Updated: 2026-01-05 (Australia/Sydney)

1. Purpose

This Privacy Statement explains how Checkout Rescue AI ("the App", "we", "us") collects, processes, and protects personal information when providing abandoned checkout recovery services to Shopify merchants.

This document describes existing system behavior only. It does not introduce new data practices beyond those already implemented in the application.

2. Role Clarity

  • Merchants are the Data Controller and Sender of Record.
  • Checkout Rescue AI acts strictly as a Data Processor and technical pipeline.
  • We do not originate marketing or communication decisions.
  • All messaging logic is merchant-configured.

3. Data Categories Processed

The App may process the following data:

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shopify customer and checkout identifiers
  • Cart contents, value, currency, timestamps
  • Contact attempt status, consent state, suppression status

4. Purpose Limitation

Data is processed solely for:

  • Abandoned checkout recovery
  • Consent, suppression, and compliance enforcement
  • Delivery auditing and merchant reporting

Checkout Rescue AI does not sell personal data, use it for advertising, or train general-purpose AI models using merchant data.

5. Voice and AI Processing

When voice recovery is enabled, calls are executed on behalf of the merchant. AI behavior is constrained, auditable, and limited to checkout recovery tasks.

6. Data Retention

  • Data is retained only as long as operationally required.
  • Retention is deterministic and policy-governed.
  • No silent or indefinite retention occurs.

7. Data Subject Rights

The App supports data access, correction, deletion, consent withdrawal, and suppression enforcement in accordance with GDPR and Australian privacy laws.

8. Security Safeguards

  • Role-based access control
  • Environment isolation
  • Audit logging and fail-closed enforcement

9. Third-Party Processors

At the merchant’s discretion, the App may integrate with email, SMS, and voice service providers. Only the minimum data required is shared.

10. Data Location

Data may be processed in jurisdictions required to operate the service. Reasonable safeguards apply regardless of location.

11. Children’s Data

The App is not intended for use with data relating to children and does not knowingly process children’s personal information.

12. Changes

This Privacy Statement may be updated to reflect legal or product changes. Material changes will be communicated via the App or listing.

13. Contact

Privacy-related enquiries should be directed via the support contact details listed in the Shopify App Store.
